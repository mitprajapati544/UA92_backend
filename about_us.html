Riston Academy

"Ristom Academy was founded in 1995 with the goal of providing a high-quality education to students in our community. Our mission is to empower students to become critical thinkers, lifelong learners, and responsible citizens."

Message from the Principal:

"As the principal of Ristom Academy School, I am proud to lead a team of dedicated educators who are committed to providing a safe and nurturing learning environment for our students. We believe in a student-centered approach to education that encourages creativity, collaboration, and critical thinking."

Faculty and Staff:

Our faculty and staff members are experts in their fields and are committed to providing the highest quality education to our students. They are dedicated to fostering a love of learning and encouraging students to reach their full potential.

Curriculum and Programs:

Our curriculum is designed to challenge and engage students, with a focus on developing critical thinking skills and promoting academic excellence. We offer a wide range of programs and extracurricular activities, including athletics, music, drama, clubs, and student organizations.

Facilities and Resources:

Our state-of-the-art facilities provide students with access to the latest technology, as well as comfortable and welcoming learning spaces. We have a fully equipped science lab, computer lab, library, and gymnasium, among other resources.

Student Body:

Our diverse student body is made up of learners from a variety of backgrounds and cultures. Our students are engaged and enthusiastic about learning, and they consistently achieve outstanding results.

Community Involvement:

We are committed to being an active member of our community, and we encourage our students to become involved in community service initiatives. We partner with local organizations to provide our students with opportunities to make a positive impact in their community.

Accreditation and Affiliations:

Ristom Academy School is accredited by the Middle States Association of Colleges and Schools and is affiliated with a number of professional organizations, including the National Association of Independent Schools.
